Rice Beef

Brief Description

A hearty dish of fluffy white rice served with rich, well-seasoned beef stew cooked in
tomatoes, onions, and spices. It’s filling, flavorful, and commonly enjoyed as a main meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 300–400 g beef (cubed)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Garlic & ginger (optional)
  • Salt, spices, oil, water

Steps

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear.
  2. Boil rice with water + pinch salt (~15 min) until tender; set aside.
  3. Boil beef separately until slightly soft.
  4. Fry onions in oil until golden.
  5. Add garlic/ginger, then tomatoes → cook to thick paste.
  6. Add boiled beef + some broth, salt, spices.
  7. Simmer 10–15 mins until stew thickens.
  8. Serve hot beef stew over rice.
