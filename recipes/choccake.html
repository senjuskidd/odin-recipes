Chocolate Cake

Brief Description

A soft, sweet baked dessert made with cocoa powder, flour, sugar, eggs, and milk. It has a rich
chocolate flavor and is enjoyed plain or with frosting.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oil/butter
  • Vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease baking tin lightly.
  3. Mix dry ingredients in bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla.
  5. Mix until smooth batter forms.
  6. Pour into baking tin.
  7. Bake ~30–40 mins (toothpick clean).
  8. Cool before slicing or frosting.
