Ugali Mayai

Brief Description

A simple, filling meal consisting of firm maize flour (ugali) served with scrambled eggs cooked with
onions and tomatoes. Common as a budget-friendly and energy-rich dish.

Ingredients

  • Maize flour (unga)
  • Water
  • Eggs (2–4)
  • Onion, tomato
  • Salt, oil

Steps

  1. Boil water in sufuria.
  2. Add maize flour gradually while stirring.
  3. Stir until thick → cook 5 mins (ugali ready).
  4. Fry onions in oil till golden.
  5. Add tomatoes → cook soft.
  6. Beat eggs with salt; pour into pan.
  7. Scramble until cooked.
  8. Serve eggs with hot ugali.
