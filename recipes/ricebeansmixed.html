Rice Beans (Mixed)

Brief Description

A nutritious one-pot meal where rice is cooked together with beans, onions, and tomatoes.
The beans add protein and texture, making it both satisfying and balanced.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup beans (boiled)
  • Onion, tomato
  • Oil, salt, spices

Steps

  1. Boil beans until soft (or use pre-boiled).
  2. Fry onions in oil till golden.
  3. Add tomatoes → cook till soft.
  4. Add beans + salt + spices; mix well.
  5. Add washed rice + water (usually 1:2 rice/water ratio).
  6. Stir once, cover tightly.
  7. Cook on low heat ~15–20 mins.
  8. Fluff gently and serve.
