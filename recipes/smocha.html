Smocha (Smokie + Chapati)

Brief Description

A popular Kenyan street snack made by wrapping a smokie (sausage) inside a soft
chapati with fresh vegetables and sauces. It’s quick, affordable, and very flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Smokies (sausages)
  • Chapati
  • Tomato, onion, cabbage (kachumbari)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Ketchup/chili sauce

Steps

  1. Boil or fry smokies until heated through.
  2. Warm chapati slightly.
  3. Place smokie on chapati.
  4. Add chopped tomato, onion, cabbage.
  5. Add sauces as desired.
  6. Roll chapati tightly around smokie.
  7. Slice halfway (optional).
  8. Serve hot.
